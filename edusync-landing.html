11 Powerful Modules

Built for every corner of school life

From enrolment to exam results, every workflow is covered — securely and efficiently.

Organisation & Admin

Create school organisations, manage profiles, run academic year rollups, and bulk promote students.

Teacher Management

Teacher registration, join-request workflows, admin verification, and classroom ownership.

Student Module

Student enrolment, class browsing, join requests, and individual attendance & result views.

Classroom & Academics

Manage classrooms, subjects, lesson plans, completion tracking, and weekly timetables.

Join Requests

Real-time pending request queues, automated approval engine, and full history for auditing.

Attendance & Leave

Digital registers, attendance trends, leave applications with teacher review and FCM notifications.

Notices & Notes

Org-wide announcements, classroom notices with Cloudinary attachments, and teacher remarks.

Examinations & Results

CA templates, mark entry, feedback recording, and aggregated performance report generation.

Push Notifications

FCM-powered real-time alerts for join requests, leave status, and notices. Persistent in-app history.

Security & Auth

JWT sessions, Bcrypt password hashing, license gating (Standard/Active/Expired), and rate limiting.

Multi-Tenant Architecture

Strict data isolation — every user only sees data belonging to their own organisation. Fully scalable.

🏫

Admin

Full control over your school's digital infrastructure — from setup to year-end rollup.

  • Create & manage the school organisation
  • Approve teachers & manage staff
  • Publish org-wide notices
  • Generate & manage license keys
  • View school-wide metrics & stats

Teacher

Manage classrooms, lessons, and student progress — all from the app.

  • Take & edit daily attendance
  • Manage subjects & lesson plans
  • Review & approve leave requests
  • Post classroom notices
  • Enter exam marks & feedback

Student

Stay on top of classes, attendance, leave, and exam results in one place.

  • Browse & join classes
  • View personal attendance record
  • Apply for leave with reasons
  • View exam results & reports
  • Receive real-time notifications
